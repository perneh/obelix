# Category 240 – Radar Video Transmission

**Edition:** 1.3 (Eurocontrol Part 14)  
**Purpose:** Transmission of monoradar primary video (radial video cells) between sensor and SDPS/display systems.

## Overview

Category 240 carries **radar video** — amplitude samples along a radial, grouped into video cells with common azimuth extent and range resolution. Obelix supports the two standard message types from the default UAP:

| Type | Name | Typical use |
|------|------|-------------|
| **1** | Video Summary | ASCII metadata describing the video stream |
| **2** | Video message | One radial with header, resolution, counters, and cell block |

Baltic scenarios emit a **video summary** after the north marker, then a **video radial** aligned with the sector-crossing azimuth (with motion sweeping the beam).

## Implemented data items

| Item | FRN | Obelix field | Notes |
|------|-----|--------------|-------|
| I240/010 Data Source Identifier | 1 | `data_source` | Mandatory SAC/SIC |
| I240/000 Message Type | 2 | `message_type` | 1=Summary, 2=Video |
| I240/020 Video Record Header | 3 | `video_sequence` | Video messages only |
| I240/030 Video Summary | 4 | `video_summary` | Summary messages only |
| I240/040 Video Header Nano | 5 | `video_header` | When `header_format` = `nano` |
| I240/041 Video Header Femto | 6 | `video_header` | When `header_format` = `femto` |
| I240/048 Resolution & Compression | 7 | `video_resolution` | RES 1–6, compression bit |
| I240/049 Octets & Cells Counters | 8 | (auto from block) | Derived at encode time |
| I240/050 Video Block Low | 9 | `video_cells_hex` | When `video_block_format` = `low` |
| I240/051 Video Block Medium | 10 | `video_cells_hex` | 64 octets per cell |
| I240/052 Video Block High | 11 | `video_cells_hex` | 256 octets per cell |
| I240/140 Time of Day | 12 | `time_of_day` | Optional on video messages |

### Video header (Nano)

- **Start/end azimuth:** degrees, LSB = 360/2¹⁶
- **Start range:** index of first cell (0 = radar location)
- **Cell duration:** nanoseconds (LSB = 1 ns)

### Video cells (low block)

Each cell is **4 octets** (32 bits). Leave `video_cells_hex` empty to use a built-in synthetic ramp (8 cells). Otherwise supply concatenated hex (8 digits per cell for low block).

### Resolution (I240/048)

| RES | Resolution |
|-----|------------|
| 1 | Monobit |
| 2 | 2 bits |
| 3 | 4 bits |
| 4 | 8 bits (default in scenarios) |
| 5 | 16 bits |
| 6 | 32 bits |
